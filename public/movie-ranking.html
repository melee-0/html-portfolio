The Best Movies According to Melissa

My top 5 movies of all-time.

Eternal Sunshine of a Spotless Mind

The acting is spot-on. The story's emotions sneak up on you in a heartbreaking fashion.

Little Miss Sunshine

Great ensemble! Quirky storyline that somehow makes you root for this dysfunctional family at the end.

The Royal Tenenbaums

What can I say? I got a thing for great ensemble movies. This was a perfect dark comedy.

Finding Nemo

I still regard this as one of Pixar's absolute best films.

Lion King

A prime example of Disney's Golden Era. Beautiful music, surprising and inspiring story. Just perfect.
